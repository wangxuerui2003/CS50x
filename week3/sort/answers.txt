sort1 uses: Bubble sort

How do you know?: The Omega value (sorted numbers) and Big O values are roughly the same.

sort2 uses: Merge sort

How do you know?: It takes a lot less time than sort 1 and 3 for both random and reversed
and the Omega value (sorted numbers) and Big O values are roughly the same.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: The Omega value (sorted numbers) is a lot greater than the Big O value.
